live.linuX-gamers.net Live CD/DVD - 0.9.6

IMPORTANT INFO

  • To start an application, either click the icon or right click on the desktop
  • To get your Internet connection working (in case it isn't), you need to start Wicd (right click menu) and enter the proper settings
  • In case sound isn't working or too quiet/loud, fiddle around with 'alsamixer' (again, right click menu)
  • If the panels are blocking, right click and choose 'Hide panels'
  • Some games might cause your screen to go black telling you "Out of range" or something along those lines. Should that happen, try and press ALT-ENTER to get out of fullscreen. If that does not work, you will have to press CTRL-ALT-BACKSPACE, login as 'gamer' and type 'startx'.
